About

This site was created to reveal the little-known history of women who have contributed to science, often in the shadows, through rigorous work combining research, testimonies, and education.

It is run by a passionate team committed to equality, recognition of female contributions, and the dissemination of accessible and respectful content.

Our ambition is to promote the understanding and appreciation of women in science today, inspiring new generations.
