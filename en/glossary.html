Glossary

Term Definition
Persona Fictional representation of a target user created based on research and data to understand their needs, behaviors, and expectations.
SWOT Strategic analysis identifying Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats of a project.
DEEPLIST External environment analysis method based on eight criteria: Demographic, Economic, Ecological, Political, Legal, Informational, Sociocultural, and Technological.
Moodboard Visual board grouping images, colors, and fonts to define the graphic identity of a project.
Tree structure Hierarchical diagram illustrating the structure or logical organization of a project, site, or folder.
Story-board Narrative tool describing a sequence of actions or screens to visualize the progression of project steps.
User journey Representation of the steps taken by a user to achieve a goal (from discovery to loyalty).
Benchmark Comparative study of existing solutions on the market to identify best practices and areas for improvement.
Specification document Contractual document gathering the objectives, functional specifications, and constraints of a project.
Deliverable Concrete element delivered to the client or evaluator at the end of a work phase (e.g., document, visual, mockup).
GANTT Chart Planning tool that allows visualization of the timeline and duration of tasks in a project.
Constraint table Document listing the technical, financial, temporal, or legal limitations of a project.
Brief Initial document that defines the expectations, targets, means, and key messages of a communication project.
Prototype/Mockup Visual or interactive representation of a future product, interface, or communication medium.
